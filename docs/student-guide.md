# Student User Guide - SkillUp Platform

Welcome to SkillUp! This comprehensive guide will help you navigate the platform, discover courses, track your learning progress, and make the most of your educational journey.

## üéØ Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Platform**
   - Go to [skillup.com](https://skillup.com)
   - Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation

2. **Registration Process**
   - Enter your email address
   - Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Provide your full name
   - Select your learning interests
   - Agree to terms and conditions
   - Click "Create Account"

3. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email for a verification link
   - Click the link to activate your account
   - Return to the platform and log in

### Profile Setup

#### Personal Information
- **Profile Picture**: Upload a professional photo
- **Bio**: Write a brief description of your background and goals
- **Location**: Add your city/country for networking
- **Learning Goals**: Specify what you want to achieve

#### Learning Preferences
- **Skill Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
- **Learning Pace**: Fast-track, Regular, or Self-paced
- **Preferred Format**: Video, Text, Interactive, or Mixed
- **Time Availability**: Hours per week you can dedicate
